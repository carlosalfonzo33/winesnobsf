The Applegate Valley in Southern Oregon

Only Hours From Napa, but a World Away.

Posted by Samantha Marie & Carlos on September 24, 2018

One of the first stops we made in Applegate Valley was Troon Vineyards. This Vineyard is one of my favorites to visit in the Applegate Valley. The scenery and property is beautiful and the wine is amazing. A big thank you to Miranda at Troon for being so helpful and amazing with introducing us to all the wines this vineyard has to offer. I was instantly sold on becoming a member!

Troon is a must visit when visting Applegate Valley wine country

Our next stop on our tour of Applegate Valley was Schmidt Family Vineyards.

What stood out to me the most was the ambience and the scenic open area. I visited this winery twice, once during the day and once at night and I can't decide wheather Schmidt Family vineyards is more beautiful by day or by night? One can give you a checkmate argument for each side.

The grounds are beautiful, the building built with great attention to detail, and the location is stunning. Inside the tasting room you have an amazing picture window to enjoy the view. There is ample seating available outside to sit in the sun and enjoy the view.

For those who have seen the Earth from space, and for the hundreds and perhaps thousands more who will, the experience most certainly changes your perspective. The things that we share in our world are far more valuable than those which divide us.
